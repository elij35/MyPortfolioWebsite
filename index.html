01About Me

Hi, I'm Eli Bowen, a Computer Science graduate from the University of Plymouth, specialising in Cyber Security with a 2:1 classification. My academic work and part-time roles have given me hands-on experience in penetration testing, network security, threat detection, programming, and technical report writing.

I have a strong interest in both offensive and defensive security, with particular enthusiasm for red teaming, blue teaming, and penetration testing. This dual focus allows me to approach challenges with a balanced perspective—identifying vulnerabilities while also developing effective defensive strategies.

Alongside my studies, I work with Cyber PATH, a police-led initiative supporting small and medium-sized organisations. In this role, I conduct OSINT investigations, vulnerability assessments, and web application security checks, and I supervise fellow students delivering security awareness training to clients. This experience has strengthened my technical expertise, leadership skills, and ability to explain complex concepts clearly to audiences with varying levels of technical knowledge.

Beyond technology, I am passionate about community engagement. I volunteer at my local food bank and, more recently, have spent a fair amount of my free time motorbiking and hiking— exploring the outdoors helps to keep me grounded and energised.
